Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed by Apple through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings under your Apple ID subscriptions.

Restore Purchases

If your subscription is active but premium features are not available, open LumoChat and use Restore Purchases from the subscription or profile area.

Account deletion

You can delete your account directly in the LumoChat app from the profile or settings area.

Translation issues

LumoChat uses AI translation. Translations may sometimes be inaccurate, incomplete, or use the wrong tone. If a translation seems important, confirm the meaning with the other person before relying on it.
