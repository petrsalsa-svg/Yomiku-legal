Common support topics

  • Creating and accepting invite links.
  • Subscription plans and chat limits.
  • Restore Purchases.
  • Push notifications.
  • Message delivery states such as sent, delivered, and read.
  • Deleting your account.
  • Translation quality or incorrect translations.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed by Apple through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings under your Apple ID subscriptions.

Restore Purchases

If your subscription is active but premium features are not available, open Yomiku and use Restore Purchases from the subscription or profile area.

Account deletion

You can delete your account directly in the Yomiku app from the profile or settings area.

Translation issues

Yomiku uses AI translation. Translations may sometimes be inaccurate, incomplete, or use the wrong tone. If a translation seems important, confirm the meaning with the other person before relying on it.
